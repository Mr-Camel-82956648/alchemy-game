#### 万军丛中跑得最快的基础怪组（强调数量与小体型）

**1. 缝合盲尸 (Stitch-Ghoul)**

- **设计意图**：最基础的炮灰。用大面积的暗红色暗血肉色块，在暗黑背景下会非常密集。
- **Prompt**: `A terrifying hunched stitch-ghoul creature, pale rotten flesh patched together with crude iron staples, no eyes, gaping maw, dragging its abnormally long arms, dark crimson red and muddy gray color tones, aggressive posture, pure white background.`

**2. 疫病鼠工 (Plague Scavenger)**

- **设计意图**：移速快的烦人脆皮。背着发光的绿毒罐子，玩家在成群怪物中一眼就能看到那一抹荧光绿。
- **Prompt**: `A small grotesque mutant rat-man scavenger, wearing cracked leather goggles, carrying a glowing fluorescent green toxic glass tank on its back, holding a rusty jagged dagger, toxic green and dirty brown color palette, pure white background.`

**3. 废料软泥 (Slag Ooze)**

- **设计意图**：割草游戏必备的史莱姆变种，但结合了炼金主题，它吞噬了废铁和骨头。
- **Prompt**: `A massive living blob of dark toxic sludge, semi-transparent sickly yellow and green slime, with rusted gears, iron chains, and glowing cracked skulls floating inside its gelatinous body, amorphous shape, pure white background.`

#### 构成中坚力量的近战军团（强调护甲与压迫感）

**4. 炉火苦工 (Furnace Thrall)**

- **设计意图**：高血怪。身体就是个火炉，胸口冒着橙色火光，能点亮游戏暗黑的场景。
- **Prompt**: `A hulking dark iron golem thrall, an open glowing orange furnace in its chest burning with intense hellfire, charred black molten-rock skin, wielding a massive scorched iron hammer, emitting embers, high contrast orange and black, pure white background.`

**5. 空洞魔铠 (Hollow Purple Armor)**

- **设计意图**：中型坦克。对应主角图3法杖上的紫球，这种怪物是被紫色炼金魔力驱动的空心铠甲。
- **Prompt**: `A floating set of rusted dark gothic plate armor, hollow inside with no body, animated by swirling glowing purple magical energy seeping from the joints and helmet visor, holding a huge jagged greatsword, deep violet and rusted iron colors, pure white background.`

**6. 骨牢恶汉 (Bone-Cage Brute)**

- **设计意图**：非常占地方的巨型怪，用来阻挡玩家走位。身上缠绕着粗大的铁链。
- **Prompt**: `A massive deformed brute, its torso encased in a crude cage made of giant white ribs and heavy black iron chains, pale dead skin, huge muscular asymmetrical arms ready to smash, horrifying dark fantasy creature design, pure white background.`

#### 躲在后排的烦人远程怪（需要鲜明的发光点吸引仇恨）

**7. 悬浮魔眼 (Alchemy Beholder)**

- **设计意图**：飘在天上的机械生物，发射射线。黄铜色金属反光能在怪海中脱颖而出。
- **Prompt**: `A floating mechanical eye monster, encased in ornate brass and copper gothic clockwork machinery, a giant glowing crimson red organic eye in the center, dangling electrical wires and chains beneath it, steampunk dark fantasy, pure white background.`

**8. 盲眼邪教徒 (Blind Cultist)**

- **设计意图**：放火球的法师怪。深蓝色的斗篷。
- **Prompt**: `A sinister floating dark fantasy cultist, wearing a tattered deep blue hooded robe, face hidden in shadows, skeletal pale hands casting a glowing cyan blue magical spell from a cursed open grimmoire, eerie atmosphere, pure white background.`

**9. 提灯幽魂 (Lantern Wraith)**

- **设计意图**：半透明的灵体，物理攻击打上去可能不疼，具有漂浮感。
- **Prompt**: `A hovering ghostly wraith figure wrapped in tattered dark shadows, no legs, holding an ornate gothic lantern radiating a bright sickly cyan-green light, ethereal smoke trailing from its body, spectral undead fantasy design, pure white background.`

#### 极具压迫感的小Boss / 精英怪（体积大，视觉极其霸道）

**10. 深渊处刑官 (Abyssal Executioner)**

- **设计意图**：精英怪。头上戴着尖刺兜帽，手里的武器比人还大，只要他出来，整个屏幕的重点都在他身上。
- **Prompt**: `An imposing demonic giant executioner, wearing a blood-stained dark leather apron and a rusted iron pyramid helmet, highly muscular dark grey skin, wielding a colossal oversized meat-cleaver axe dripping with dark magic, final boss energy, pure white background.`
